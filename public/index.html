Services

Discover the transformative features that make Dira Farm the trusted platform for empowering farmers, improving crop productivity, and advancing sustainable agriculture...

AI-Powered Crop Diagnosis

Diagnose potato leaf health using AI models trained on a vast dataset. Users can upload images to receive accurate classifications, distinguishing between healthy leaves, early blight, and late blight for timely interventions.

Treatment Recommendations

Get actionable insights with detailed treatment plans for detected diseases, including medication suggestions and step-by-step procedures, tailored to improve crop health and reduce losses.

Educational Resources

Access free, curated educational materials to learn sustainable farming practices, disease prevention strategies, and efficient crop management techniques, empowering farmers to boost productivity.

Nearby Agrovet Locator

Find agrovet shops near you for quick access to recommended medications, tools, and resources, ensuring timely implementation of treatment plans.

User-Friendly Interface

Enjoy an intuitive platform design with easy navigation for seamless interactions, from uploading images and viewing diagnoses to accessing resources and tracking your farming history.

Historical Diagnosis Tracking

Keep a record of past diagnoses and treatments to monitor farm health trends over time. This feature helps farmers make data-driven decisions to improve yields and prevent recurring issues.

About Dira Farm

Dira Farm is an AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize small-scale potato farming in Kenya. It empowers farmers with advanced tools for diagnosing crop health, offering disease-specific treatments, and providing access to educational resources. With features like agrovet locators and data-driven insights, Dira Farm fosters sustainable agriculture and enhances productivity.

Our Mission

At Dira Farm, our mission is to empower small-scale Kenyan farmers with AI tools for crop health diagnosis and sustainable farming, boosting productivity, reducing crop losses, and enhancing food security through accessible technology and education.

Our Vision

Dira Farm envisions being the leading platform empowering small-scale Kenyan farmers with technology to enhance agricultural practices, boost productivity, reduce losses, and promote sustainability, ensuring food security for all.

Our Values

At Dira Farm, we value innovation, excellence, integrity, collaboration, growth, and sustainability. We strive for creative solutions, high standards, honesty, continuous learning, and sustainable practices to ensure long-term success in agriculture.
